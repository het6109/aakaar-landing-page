About Aakaar

Aakaar is the prestigious annual technical festival of the Civil Engineering Department at IIT Bombay. It provides a national platform for students to demonstrate their skills, explore innovations, and engage with cutting-edge trends in civil engineering.

The event blends technical excellence with creativity, offering a series of competitions, symposiums, workshops, and interactive events to foster both learning and leadership.

Key Features of Aakaar:

🎤

International Civil Engineering Symposium

Showcase your research and engage with global civil engineering experts in technical paper presentations and discussions.

💻

Software Workshops

Hands-on training on tools like AutoCAD, Revit, STAAD.Pro and more — guided by professionals and educators.

🏗️

Technical Competitions

Bridge-It, Conquer-It, and CENEx — test your design and engineering acumen in real-world challenges.

🌱

Flagship Events

Engage in exciting events like CiviQ (quiz), Smart Pitch (innovation showcase), and ENVIZN (eco-focused engineering).
